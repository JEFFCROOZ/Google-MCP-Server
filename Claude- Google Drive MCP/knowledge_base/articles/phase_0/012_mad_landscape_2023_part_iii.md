## Summary
The third installment of Matt Turck's MAD (Machine Learning, AI, and Data) Landscape 2023 series focuses on the "Get Grounded" phase, examining how organizations can build durable foundations in a rapidly expanding and often chaotic data and AI ecosystem. For data leaders, the key takeaway is that amid hype and proliferation of tools, disciplined prioritization and architectural coherence matter more than chasing every emerging technology. Understanding where the landscape is consolidating versus fragmenting is essential for making defensible infrastructure and vendor decisions.

## Key Excerpts
> The MAD landscape continues to expand rapidly, creating both opportunity and noise that requires data leaders to develop strong filters for technology evaluation and adoption.
> Organizations risk "shiny object" syndrome by reacting to AI hype rather than grounding investments in clear business and data strategy fundamentals.
> Consolidation is emerging in certain layers of the data stack, suggesting that betting on durable, well-integrated platforms may outperform assembling fragmented best-of-breed toolchains.
