## Summary
The MAD (Machine learning, Artificial intelligence, and Data) Landscape 2014 maps the rapidly expanding ecosystem of big data tools, vendors, and platforms, illustrating the explosive proliferation of technologies across the data stack. For data leaders, this landscape serves as a strategic orientation tool, helping organizations understand where key capabilities reside, identify consolidation trends, and make informed vendor and build-vs-buy decisions in a fast-moving market.

## Key Excerpts
> The big data ecosystem had grown dramatically by 2014, with hundreds of vendors competing across infrastructure, analytics, and application layers of the data stack.
> Navigating the MAD landscape requires data leaders to develop a coherent architectural vision rather than reacting opportunistically to individual vendor offerings.
> Open source frameworks like Hadoop were anchoring the ecosystem, with commercial vendors building differentiated layers of tooling and services on top of foundational platforms.
