## Summary
The 2024 MAD (Machine Learning, AI & Data) Landscape maps the explosive growth and consolidation of the AI/data ecosystem, highlighting how AI has become the dominant organizing force across previously distinct data tooling categories. For data leaders, this signals a need to reassess vendor strategies and architectural decisions as traditional boundaries between analytics, ML, and AI infrastructure collapse. The landscape also reflects a maturing market where differentiation is increasingly difficult and platform consolidation is accelerating.

## Key Excerpts
> The AI wave has restructured the entire data ecosystem, with nearly every vendor reorienting their positioning and product roadmap around AI capabilities.
> Consolidation is intensifying as large platforms absorb point solutions, making build-vs-buy and vendor selection decisions more consequential for data leaders.
> The sheer volume of tools across the MAD landscape signals both innovation abundance and evaluation fatigue for teams trying to maintain coherent data architectures.
