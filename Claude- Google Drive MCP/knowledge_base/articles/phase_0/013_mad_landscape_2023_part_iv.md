## Summary
The fourth installment of Matt Turck's MAD (Machine Learning, AI, and Data) Landscape 2023 series focuses on the "Get Grounded" phase, examining how organizations can build durable foundations in their data and AI ecosystems amid rapid technological change. For data leaders, this framing is strategically important: it signals a maturing market where disciplined infrastructure choices, governance practices, and stack rationalization matter more than chasing emerging tools. The analysis helps data leaders prioritize investments that will compound over time rather than accumulate technical and organizational debt.

## Key Excerpts
> The MAD landscape has grown enormously complex, requiring data leaders to focus on foundational stack decisions rather than continuously adopting the latest emerging tools.
> Organizations that invest in grounding their data infrastructure first are better positioned to extract durable value from AI and machine learning capabilities.
> Rationalization of the data ecosystem — reducing redundancy and establishing clear ownership — is becoming a competitive differentiator for mature data organizations.
