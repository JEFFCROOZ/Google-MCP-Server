## Summary
The 2021 MAD (Machine Learning, AI, and Data) Landscape maps the explosive growth and fragmentation of the modern data ecosystem, cataloging hundreds of vendors across infrastructure, analytics, ML, and AI tooling. For data leaders, it provides a structured view of market maturity, emerging categories, and consolidation trends — essential context for making informed technology investment and architecture decisions.

## Key Excerpts
> The data and AI tooling market has grown dramatically, with new categories emerging rapidly and incumbents facing disruption from cloud-native challengers.
> Data infrastructure has shifted toward a modular, composable architecture, replacing monolithic platforms with best-of-breed tools across ingestion, storage, transformation, and serving.
> Navigating this landscape requires data leaders to balance innovation adoption with integration complexity, as vendor proliferation increases both optionality and organizational risk.
