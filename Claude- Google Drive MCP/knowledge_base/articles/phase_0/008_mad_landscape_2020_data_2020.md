## Summary
The 2020 MAD (Machine Learning, AI, and Data) Landscape maps the explosive growth and fragmentation of the data and AI tooling ecosystem, highlighting consolidation trends alongside continued proliferation of new vendors across infrastructure, analytics, and ML. For data leaders, this landscape serves as a strategic reference for vendor evaluation, stack architecture decisions, and understanding where commoditization versus differentiation is occurring across the data value chain.

## Key Excerpts
> The data and AI tooling market has grown dramatically, with hundreds of vendors competing across overlapping categories, making intentional stack curation essential for data leaders.
> Cloud hyperscalers are increasingly absorbing capabilities previously held by independent vendors, shifting the build-vs-buy calculus for enterprise data teams.
> Open source continues to drive innovation at the infrastructure layer, often preceding commercial adoption and signaling where the market is heading next.
