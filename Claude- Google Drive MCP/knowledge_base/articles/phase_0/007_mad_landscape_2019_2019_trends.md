## Summary
The MAD (Machine Learning, AI, and Data) Landscape 2019 maps the explosive growth and consolidation of the data and AI ecosystem, highlighting key trends such as the maturation of open source projects, the rise of cloud-native data infrastructure, and the increasing dominance of major platform players. For data leaders, this landscape signals the need to make deliberate architectural choices amid a crowded vendor market, distinguishing between enduring foundational tools and transient point solutions. Understanding these macro trends helps data executives prioritize investments and avoid technical debt in a rapidly shifting stack.

## Key Excerpts
> The data and AI ecosystem continues to expand rapidly, with hundreds of new vendors emerging while a smaller set of established players consolidate significant market power.
> Open source projects are increasingly becoming the backbone of enterprise data infrastructure, with commercialization following adoption rather than preceding it.
> Cloud platforms are reshaping the competitive dynamics of the MAD landscape, often commoditizing standalone tools by absorbing their functionality into managed services.
