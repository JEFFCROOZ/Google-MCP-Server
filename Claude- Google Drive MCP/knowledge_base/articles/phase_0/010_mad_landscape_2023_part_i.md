## Summary
The 2023 MAD (Machine Learning, AI & Data) Landscape maps the explosive growth and consolidation of the data and AI ecosystem, highlighting how the rise of generative AI has fundamentally reshuffled priorities and vendor relevance across the stack. For data leaders, this matters because it signals where investment is concentrating, which categories are commoditizing, and where strategic bets on infrastructure and tooling carry the most risk or opportunity.

## Key Excerpts
> The 2023 MAD landscape has grown dramatically, reflecting both the maturation of foundational data infrastructure and the disruptive emergence of large language models across every layer of the stack.
> Generative AI is not an isolated trend but a force reshaping existing data categories, forcing vendors and practitioners to reassess their positioning and product roadmaps rapidly.
> Data leaders must navigate a more complex vendor landscape where consolidation and AI-native entrants are simultaneously compressing and expanding the choices available for enterprise data platforms.
