## Summary
The *State of AI in Business 2025 Report* by MLQ.ai maps the current adoption landscape of AI across enterprise environments, highlighting where organizations are investing, where friction remains, and which capabilities are maturing fastest. For data leaders, this type of industry benchmarking is critical for calibrating data infrastructure priorities, governance frameworks, and AI readiness strategies against peer organizations. Understanding where the enterprise AI market is heading in 2025 informs decisions around data quality, model governance, and organizational AI enablement.

## Key Excerpts
> Enterprise AI adoption in 2025 is shifting from experimentation to operationalization, requiring organizations to move beyond pilots and embed AI into core business workflows.
> Data quality and governance infrastructure remain primary bottlenecks limiting organizations from scaling AI initiatives effectively across business units.
> Business leaders increasingly expect AI to deliver measurable ROI, placing pressure on data teams to demonstrate value through rigorous performance tracking and accountability frameworks.
